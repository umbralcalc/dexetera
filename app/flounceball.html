Rules of Flounceball:

1. The objective of the game is for each team to keep the ball in the air for as long as possible.

2. Possession alternates between each team, where the objective of the defensive side is to stop the ball from staying in the air by disrupting the play of the attacking side.

3. Defensive players are not allowed to touch the ball, otherwise the time the ball has remained in the air is subtracted from their team total.

4. Defensive players may tackle any attacking player within 10m radius of the lat/long ball location.

5. Player substitutions may occur continuously throughout the match and there is no limit to them.

6. The game takes place on a circular field and if the ball leaves this area at any time, then the possession of the attacking team is ended.

7. A match is 40 minutes of continuous play, without stoppages.

How to play:

You are the Flounceball team manager and tactician. You must guide your side to victory through player substitutions and continuously tweaking team tactics.

Edit/run this code below and then click the "Start Simulation" button to play:
